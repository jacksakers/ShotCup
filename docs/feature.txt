World Cup Fantasy App - Full Feature List

1. Authentication & Onboarding

Global Password Gate: One password to view the site.

Profile Picker: "Who are you?" screen to select your identity.

Add Player (Pre-Draft): Open signup form where family members can type their name to join the pool before the draft begins.

2. The Draft System (Admin Controlled)

Random Team Allocation: Admin presses a button to randomly distribute the 32 remaining teams evenly among all registered family members.

Edge Case Handling: If 32 doesn't divide evenly by the number of players, the remaining teams are assigned to the "House" (unowned), or left as free agents.

3. User Dashboard & Leaderboard

Global Leaderboard: Ranks family members by total points.

Team Roster View: Clicking a family member shows the teams they own and how many points each team has contributed.

Mobile-First UI: Large tap targets, clean tables, and swipeable views for mobile phones.

4. Trading System

Trade Market: A screen showing all teams and their owners.

Propose Trade: User A selects their team and requests a team from User B.

Trade Inbox: Users get an alert when they log in if they have a pending trade. They can Accept or Reject.

Trade History: A feed showing recent trades (e.g., "Dad traded USA to Mom for France").

5. Scoring System (Configurable)

Since this starts at the Round of 32, points should be weighted for knockout success:

Match Win: +3 points

Match Draw (Penalties): +1 point

Goals Scored: +1 point per goal

Clean Sheet: +2 points

Advance to Quarter-Finals: +3 points

Advance to Semi-Finals: +5 points

Advance to Final: +7 points

Win World Cup: +10 points

6. Admin Panel (Secure)

Standings Importer: A large <textarea> where the admin pastes the raw text from the FIFA website.

Match Result Importer: (For Knockouts) A simple form to input specific game results (e.g., USA 2 - 1 Mexico) to trigger advance bonuses.

Manual Override: Ability to manually edit a team's score or a user's points in case of errors.

Trade Veto: Admin ability to cancel or reverse accidental trades.
